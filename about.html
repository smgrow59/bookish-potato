Meet The Taste Testers!

Pyrrha

A picture-perfect picky eating husky!
The reason I had to start baking my own treats.

Axl

A very hungry chihuahua terrier mix
that eats anything that's not good for him!

Dotty

A little Iggy who is selective about
what treats she steals from Axl.

These doggies are my world. One thing I will always be passionate about is advocating for dog health. When I looked at the treats
I was giving my babies, I saw a long list of ingredients that I either couldn't pronounce or weren't good for longterm health.
And so started the homemade journey! While homemade dog food is incredibly hard to get right and could lead to a lack of
micronutrients, treats are harder to get wrong. I one day hope to sell these treats in small batches, we are still
in the refinment stage and are working on solidifying recipies. Here's to what the future holds!
Hopefully my dogs will thank me.
